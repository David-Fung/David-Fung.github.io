About Us

Dream Fitness are fitness center chains located throughout New York City.
Our goal is to promote good health by encouraging everyone to exercise at our gym.
We offer high quality equipments, fitness courses, and one-to-one personal trainer service.


What We Offer


The Finest Equipment

Dream Fitness offers the finest gym equipments for the finest results. We offer a variety of equipments for cardiovascular workouts and weight training workouts.

Personal Trainer

Our personal training program offers a one-to-one personal trainer who will motivate you, monitor your changes, and provide accurate feedback for optimal improvement. Our professional trainers are specialized in exercise physiology and nutrition.

Fitness Programs

If you are looking to relax your mind, perform less intensive exercises, or to increase your overall flexibility, our yoga program is for you! We also offer boxing and swimming programs for those interested in highly intense cardio exercises.
